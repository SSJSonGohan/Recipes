Japanese Curry Recipe

Japanese curry....mmmmmm.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 glove of ginger
  • 300g~ of chicken legs, or choice of meat
  • 300g of potatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 20g butter
  • 4 tablespoon of flour
  • 3 tablespoon of curry powder
  • 200 mL of vegetable juice/stock
  • 600 ml of water
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • 2 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon of instant coffee
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of worcester(worcetershire) sauce

Steps

  1. Slice the onion and place into a pan.
  2. Wash and peel the carrot. Then cut into bite sized pieces.
  3. Peel and cut teh potatoes. Then soak them in water.
  4. Peel and sliced the ginger very thinly.
  5. Chop up the garlic.
  6. Place ginger and garlic into the same pot thatthe onions are in and fry until the onions are goden brown.
  7. Add salt and butter.
  8. Add the flour.
  9. Mix on low heat until smooth.
  10. Add the curry powder and mix until smooth. Then add the vegetable juice/
  11. Add 3 cups of water.
  12. Add teh ketchup, worcester sauce, and instant coffee.
  13. Simmer over low heat for 20 minutes with lid open.
  14. Cut the chicken into bite sized pieces.
  15. In a large pan, fry the chicken, then in the same pan fry the carrots and potatoes.
  16. Place teh contents of teh pan into the pot and bring to a boil.
